Cebu Technological University - Danao CampusCTU

CTU is a public, non-sectarian, coeducational, state-funded higher education institution located in Cebu, Philippines. The CTU system counts a total of 23 campuses, which consists of one main campus in Cebu City, nine satellite campuses, and 13 extension campuses. As of school year 2019 - 2020, there are 41,395 students enrolled on the 23 CTU campuses located throughout the province of Cebu

College of EngineeringCOE

The College of Engineering at CTU - Danao is committed to delivering high-quality education, hands-on training, and research prospects to cultivate skilled engineers capable of driving technological advancements and meeting industry demands.

College of Management and EntrepreneurshipCME

The College of Management and Entrepreneurship at CTU -Danao provides students with comprehensive education and practical skills in the fields of management and entrepreneurship to prepare them for successful careers in business, leadership, and innovative ventures.

College of TechnologyCOT

The College of Technology at CTU-Danao aims to equip students with the necessary skills and knowledge to meet the demands of the ever-evolving technology industry. The programs integrate theoretical learning with practical training to ensure students are well-prepared for careers in their respective fields.

College of Education Arts and SciencesCEAS

The College of Education Arts and Sciences at CTU-Danao provides students with the knowledge, skills, and experiences necessary to become effective teachers, administrators, researchers, counselors, or other education-related professionals. It also conducts research to improve educational practices and inform policy decisions.

GOAL

By the end of 2024, CTU will be globally recognized for its quality. Specifically, it will:
1. Obtain a Philippine university system status with a CHED SUC Level V recognition or equivalent;
2. Be ranked among the Top 10 Universities in the Philippines;
3. Be included in the list of internationally recognized ranking of universities (including Quacquarelli Symonds.Times Higher Education. Shanghai University Ranking. World University Impact Rankings);
4. Pursue efforts to pave way for the development of new programs (including natural sciences and Doctor of Medicine) and Transnational Higher Education;
5. Achieve world-class performance and excellence in total quality management; and
6. Lead Innovations in the Fourth Industrial Revolution.

OBJECTIVE

1. Ensure relevant, effective, and quality education to achieve sustainable growth.
2. Increase access to quality education for the underprivileged students.
3. Produce relevant research to promote economic, environmental and sustainable development.
4. Expand community engagement.
5. Strengthen effective and efficient management of resources.
